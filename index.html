FUNIVIA

by Gaurav

Instructions:

  • Start your game by pressing Play button on Home screen.
  • Each game contains 10 fun questions for you.
  • You will be provided with a time for each game.
  • You have to answer each question to proceed for the next question.
  • You can check your result after you submit all answers or time's up.
  • Each correct Question fetches you 5 points.
  • Each wrong Question fetches you a penalty of 5 points i.e. -5 points. (ouch!)

* Funivia is under construction.
